How Claude + Skills Works

A three-stage pipeline combining vision AI with domain expertise for insurance-grade accuracy.

1

Vision Analysis

Claude's multimodal capabilities interpret form images, understanding layout, handwriting, and document structure regardless of format.

2

Skill-Guided Extraction

Domain-specific skills inject insurance knowledge, field validation rules, and industry terminology for precise data capture.

3

Compliance Validation

Extracted data is validated against state-specific regulatory requirements with full statutory citation trails.

Built-In Regulatory Intelligence

Unlike generic document AI, our skills embed actual insurance regulations directly into the extraction process.

Skills Know the Law

Each extraction skill contains embedded regulatory knowledge from actual state insurance codes, ensuring every field is validated against current compliance requirements.
